Equinox

It's good to see you old friend,
O how the time has flown,
The summer sun has come and gone and left me all alone.

I have missed you dear fellow,
Far more than words can say,
For when would life without such strife be fit to greet the day?

Once upon a time you know,
I might have cursed your name,
But now I see this loss of me is really just a game.

Or so you say, parasite,
Don't play your games with me,
If you must win then I must lose yet still you lose with me.

'That is not dead which can eternal lie. And with strange aeons even death may die.' - H. P. Lovecraft
